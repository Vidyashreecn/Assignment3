Bitter Gourd Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium bitter gourds (karela), thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Bitter Gourd

Remove Seeds and Slice: Cut the bitter gourds lengthwise and remove the seeds. Slice them thinly and soak them in salted water for 15-20 minutes to reduce bitterness. Drain and rinse before using.

Step 2: Cook the Bitter Gourd

Sauté Bitter Gourd: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the sliced bitter gourd and sauté until they start to brown. Remove them from the pan and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Curry Base

Sauté Onions and Tomatoes: In the same pan, add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute. Then, add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.

Step 4: Add Spices

Add Spice Powders: Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt to the onion-tomato mixture. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes until the spices are fragrant.

Step 5: Combine and Cook

Add Bitter Gourd: Return the sautéed bitter gourd slices to the pan and mix well with the onion-tomato-spice mixture. Add a splash of water if the mixture looks too dry. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes until the bitter gourd is tender.

Step 6: Final Touch

Add Garam Masala: Once the bitter gourd is cooked through, sprinkle garam masala over the curry and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 7: Serve

Garnish and Serve: Garnish the Bitter Gourd Curry with chopped cilantro leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
