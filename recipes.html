Butter Chicken

A classic North Indian dish, Butter Chicken is made with marinated chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato sauce. The dish is known for its vibrant color and buttery flavor, often enhanced with a blend of spices such as garam masala, cumin, and coriander. It's usually served with naan or rice.

Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular vegetarian appetizer made from chunks of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and grilled until charred and smoky. It is often served with mint chutney and garnished with lemon wedges and onion rings.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), and a blend of aromatic spices. Often layered and cooked in a sealed pot (dum style), biryani is typically garnished with fried onions, boiled eggs, and fresh herbs. Variations include Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and Kolkata Biryani, each with its unique twist.

Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian street food, Chole Bhature consists of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). The chickpea curry is made with a blend of spices and is often garnished with fresh coriander, chopped onions, and a squeeze of lime.

Masala Dosa

Originating from South India, Masala Dosa is a crispy, thin crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato filling. It is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, a tangy lentil soup.

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a flavorful curry from Kashmir, traditionally made with tender pieces of lamb or goat cooked in a rich gravy of yogurt, garlic, and various spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. The dish is known for its deep red color, which comes from dried Kashmiri chilies.

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a nutritious and delicious dish made from paneer cubes simmered in a creamy spinach gravy. The dish is mildly spiced and is a staple in many Indian households. It's often served with naan, roti, or rice.

Samosa

Samosas are popular Indian snacks made from a thin pastry dough filled with a savory mixture of mashed potatoes, peas, and spices, then deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often served with tamarind chutney and mint chutney.

Dal Makhani

A beloved dish from Punjab, Dal Makhani is made with whole black lentils and kidney beans slowly cooked with butter, cream, and a blend of spices. It has a rich, creamy texture and is best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food from Mumbai, Pav Bhaji is a spicy vegetable mash served with buttered and toasted bread rolls (pav). The vegetable curry is made with potatoes, peas, tomatoes, and a special blend of spices, garnished with a dollop of butter, chopped onions, and fresh coriander.
