Rava Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine semolina, rice flour, all-purpose flour, chopped onions, green chili, ginger, cilantro, cumin seeds, whole black peppercorns, and salt. Mix well

Step 2: Make the Batter

Add Water: Gradually add water to the dry ingredients while whisking continuously to form a smooth, thin batter. The consistency should be similar to that of buttermilk. Let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes.

Step 3: Cook the Dosa

Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick dosa tawa (griddle) on medium-high heat. Once hot, sprinkle a few drops of water on the pan and wipe it off with a cloth or paper towel to create a slightly damp surface. Pour the Batter: Stir the batter well before pouring. Take a ladleful of batter and pour it onto the center of the tawa in a circular motion, starting from the outer edges and filling in the gaps. Do not spread the batter like a regular dosa. Drizzle Oil: Drizzle some vegetable oil around the edges and on the surface of the dosa. Cook the Dosa: Cook the dosa on medium-high heat until the edges turn golden brown and crisp, and the surface is cooked through. No need to flip the dosa. Fold and Serve: Fold the dosa into half or roll it up and transfer it to a serving plate. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Step 4: Serve

Serve Hot: Serve the Rava Dosa hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or any chutney of your choice.
