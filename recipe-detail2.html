Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt, to taste

For the Filling:

  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Ghee or oil, for cooking

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

Make the Dough: In a large bowl, combine whole wheat flour and salt. Add water gradually and knead to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

Prepare the Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine mashed potatoes, cumin seeds, green chili, ginger, garam masala, chili powder, cilantro, and salt. Mix well.

Step 3: Stuff and Roll

Divide and Stuff: Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Flatten each ball and place a portion of the potato filling in the center. Seal the edges and gently roll out into a flatbread.

Step 4: Cook the Paratha

Cook on Tawa: Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat. Cook each paratha with ghee or oil until golden brown on both sides.

Step 5: Serve

Serve: Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter.
