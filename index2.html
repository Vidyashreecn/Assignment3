THE LATEST & GREATEST

Prawns Fry

Prawns are a loved ingredient all over southern India, either in a sauce as a main dish or as a tangy snack on their own. You can eat it with your fingers - much less formal and more fun....

Aloo Paratha

Aloo paratha is a paratha stuffed with potato filling native to the Indian subcontinent. It is traditionally eaten for breakfast. It is made using unleavened dough rolled with a mixture of mashed potato and spices which is cooked on a hot tawa with butter or ghee.

Big Yummy Italian Salad

Big Yummy Italian Salad! with salami, pepperoncini, two kinds of cheese, garlic fried panko, and a mindless dressing in every salad crevice.

Rava Dosa

Rava Dosa popular South Indian crepe recipe made with semolina, rice flour, and plain flour. The batter of semolina dosa is thin as compared to traditional rice-based dosa batter which yields crisp and flaky dosa.

Bitter gourd curry

Bitter gourd curry or karela sabzi recipe - simple dish made with bitter melon, spices and herbs. Serve it with rice, roti or chaapti.
