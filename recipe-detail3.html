Big Yummy Italian Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup pepperoncini peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Italian dressing, to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Chop and Slice: Wash and prepare all the vegetables. Chop the romaine lettuce, halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, thinly slice the red onion, slice the black olives and pepperoncini peppers, and slice the roasted red peppers.

Step 2: Assemble the Salad

Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, thinly sliced red onion, sliced black olives, sliced pepperoncini peppers, sliced roasted red peppers, torn basil leaves, chopped parsley, cubed mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 3: Season the Salad

Season with Salt and Pepper: Season the salad with salt and black pepper according to your taste preferences.

Step 4: Dress the Salad

Add Italian Dressing: Drizzle Italian dressing over the salad ingredients. Start with a small amount and add more as needed, tossing the salad gently to coat all the ingredients evenly

Step 5: Serve

Plate and Serve: Transfer the Big Yummy Italian Salad to a serving dish or individual plates. Serve immediately as a refreshing and delicious appetizer or side dish.
