About Me

Hello! I'm Vidyashree C N , hailing from the culturally rich state of Karnataka, has always been passionate about discovering the diverse tapestry of India and beyond. My travel experiences include exploring the majestic palaces of Mysore, hiking the verdant hills of Coorg, and enjoying the pristine beaches of Gokarna. My adventures extend to the streets of Paris, the serene backwaters of Kerala, and the historic monuments of Rajasthan. I embraces each journey with enthusiasm, learning about various traditions, savoring regional cuisines, and connecting with people from different walks of life, thus enriching my perspective and experiences.
